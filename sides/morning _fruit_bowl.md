# Morning fruit bowl

## Ingredients

- 1 Banana
- 50 g of grapes
- 50 g of strawberry
- 20 g of blueberry
- 20 g of rawsperry
- Muesli and white chocolate for topping


## Instructions

- Clean and cut all the fruits 
- Put everythin a bowl and top it with the muesli and white chocolate
- Enjoy! :)
